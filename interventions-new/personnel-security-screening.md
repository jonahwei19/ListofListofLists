# Personnel security screening for AI access

**Tag**: Security

## What it is
Mandated screening and monitoring for personnel with access to powerful AI systems, analogous to security clearances in intelligence or cybersecurity. Primary goal is eliminating worst outcomes from geopolitical adversaries or mentally unstable individuals accessing AGI-level systems.

## Why it matters
- Insider threats are hardest to defend against
- Screening reduces probability of bad actor access
- Trades off against talent acquisition and organizational culture

## Current state
- **Status**: Idea
- **Bottlenecks**: Defining screening criteria; talent pipeline effects; privacy concerns; implementation costs
