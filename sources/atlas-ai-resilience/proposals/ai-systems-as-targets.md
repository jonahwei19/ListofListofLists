# AI Systems as High-Value Targets

**Source**: Atlas AI Resilience Gap Map
**URL**: (internal document from Atlas Computing)

## Quoted passage

> gap: Advanced AI systems face become high-value targets to adversaries and face significant security threats from theft, unauthorized modification, and adversarial attacks on deployed models

> claim: there is sufficient commercial interest in security, so progress is primarily limited by having an implementable standard for sufficient security

> Develop and promulgate security standards for AI datacenters (building on frameworks like RAND SL5), including training curricula and consulting services to accelerate adoption.

> claim: there is insufficient sufficient commercial interest in security, so progress should be made by nonprofit efforts

> Build reference implementation of high-security (e.g. SL5) AI datacenter to demonstrate feasibility and de-risk adoption for commercial operators
