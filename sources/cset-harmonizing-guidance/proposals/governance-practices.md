## Relevant CSET Practices (Governance Topics)

### Strategy & Leadership

#### SL-1: Organizational Strategy
> "Define an organization-wide strategy for managing technology. Develop policies that address cybersecurity, safety, privacy, and risk management. Communicate and enforce policies across the organization."

**AI Score**: 62%

#### SL-4: Integrated Risk Management
> "Integrate the range of risk management activities (AI, cybersecurity, privacy, supply chain, etc.) into the enterprise risk management program. Coordinate and align these activities with each other and with those of external partners."

**AI Score**: 50%

#### SL-5: Culture
> "Ensure that leadership demonstrates a commitment to safety, security, privacy, and accountability. Communicate openly about risks and empower individuals at all levels to report issues and concerns."

**AI Score**: 50%

### Risk Management

#### RM-4: Risk Identification
> "Identify threats, vulnerabilities, and risks to the organization."

**AI Score**: 40%

#### RM-5: Risk Assessment and Analysis
> "Assess the likelihood and magnitude of risks, quantitatively or qualitatively, accounting for uncertainty."

**AI Score**: 45%

#### RM-10: Transparency and Communication
> "Communicate information about risk, the organization's actions to mitigate them, and the effectiveness of those actions to internal and external stakeholders."

**AI Score**: 64%

### Audit & Compliance

#### AU-1: Audit Oversight
> "Create governance structures within the organization that includes establishing independent risk management and oversight functions."

**AI Score**: 60%

#### AU-3: Audit
> "Establish an independent audit and assurance function within the organization. Conduct audits regularly. Define objectives, criteria, and scope for each audit."

**AI Score**: 48%

#### AU-6: Culture and Collaboration
> "Promote an organizational culture that prioritizes safety and risk mitigation. Encourage collaboration and communication between teams, both internally and externally."

**AI Score**: 93%

#### AU-7: Communication and Disclosure
> "Communicate the organization's commitment to governance, risk management, and compliance to stakeholders. Share policies, practices, and outcomes related to these initiatives publicly."

**AI Score**: 96%

## Connection to Existing Interventions

**Intergovernmental Frontier Model Forum**: SL-4's "coordinate and align these activities with each other and with those of external partners" and AU-6's "encourage collaboration and communication between teams, both internally and externally" support international coordination.

**Government AI Expertise Centers**: RM-4 and RM-5's risk identification and assessment practices require expertise. The report notes organizations need "time, resources, and expertise that many organizations, particularly smaller ones, cannot afford."

**Government Risk Framework Expansion**: SL-1's call for "organization-wide strategy for managing technology" and SL-4's "integrated risk management" directly support government adoption of AI risk frameworks.

**AI Policy Studio**: AU-7's "share policies, practices, and outcomes related to these initiatives publicly" and RM-10's communication requirements support policy development infrastructure.
